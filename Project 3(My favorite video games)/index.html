Top 5 Movies

Topgun Maverick

This movie is a thrilling watch. No matter what you're into, this movie is bound to keep you entertained. The non stop action on the flight deck combined with the drama and humor from the summer nightlife makes for a perfect movie. The intensity of flight school and high risk missions keep you on the edge of your seat. I thought that Tom Cruise did an outstanding job in this film which really brought it all together.

Happy Gilmore

As always, Adam Sandler proves to be one of the funniest actors around. I don’t think I stopped laughing until minutes after this movie ended. This comedy is the funniest movie I have ever watched. Besides the unmatched humor this feel good story of a mother son relationship is amazing. This is one of my favorite movies of all time.

The Water Boy

Once again, an Adam Sandler movie with unreal humor. This comedy is nothing more than non-stop laughter yet still it's an all time great. As Bobby Bouchet gets discovered as a mysteriously good tackler his puny role as waterboy transforms into the star player. Unfortunately for him his mom never let him play football, so he must keep a big secret. This is a must watch movie.

Home Alone

This holiday classic is just as amazing every Christmas season. The chaotic house somehow forgets a family member at home for a week, and his journey through living alone is one of the more enjoyable things I have ever watched. I highly recommend this movie.
